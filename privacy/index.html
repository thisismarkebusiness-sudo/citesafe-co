1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you visit citesafe.co, create an account, use the product (including beta features), or contact us. Related sites (thisismarke.com, markeconsulting.online) have their own notices where linked.

We do not sell your personal information or sell the contents of your uploaded documents as a product.

4. Documents, confidentiality & retention

Treat uploads as confidential work product. Only submit documents you are authorized to process. When server-side processing is enabled, we use documents solely to provide the service (extract citations, run checks, show results). We aim for short retention and deletion tools as the product matures. Operator and MCG may retain limited logs and account records as needed for security, billing, and legal obligations.

No attorney–client privilege is created by uploading documents to CiteSafe. Do not assume privilege or work-product protection from using the tool.
